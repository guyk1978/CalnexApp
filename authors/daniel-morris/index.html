Senior Mortgage Analyst

Daniel Morris

Educational U.S. mortgage and consumer lending content—numbers-first, disclosure-aware, tool-backed.

Daniel Morris covers mortgage refinancing, home equity borrowing, amortization strategy, and consumer lending analysis for CalnexApp. He writes for readers who want mechanics-first explanations—how amortization, disclosures, and investor rules show up in payment paths, qualification, and break-even decisions. This is educational content, not a substitute for a licensed loan officer, attorney, or tax advisor.

Topics covered

  • Mortgage refinancing and break-even framing
  • ARM analysis (indexes, margins, cap ladders)
  • Home equity borrowing (HEL, HELOC, cash-out)
  • Mortgage qualification (DTI, credit pricing, pre-approval paperwork)
  • APR, points, PMI, and closing-cost disclosure literacy
  • Loan amortization, extra principal, and term trade-offs
