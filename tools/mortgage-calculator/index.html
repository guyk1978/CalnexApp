Mortgage Planning FAQ

Does this include property tax and insurance?

Yes, annual property tax and insurance are converted to monthly escrow and added to your total monthly payment.

Can I compare with a standard loan?

Yes, use the Loan Calculator to compare non-mortgage repayment scenarios.

How is affordability calculated?

It uses a simple 28% rule: recommended monthly housing payment is 28% of gross monthly income.
