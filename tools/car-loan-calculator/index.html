Car Financing FAQ

How do trade-in and down payment affect monthly cost?

Both reduce the financed balance, which lowers monthly payment and total interest.

Is a 72-month term always better because payment is lower?

Not always. Longer terms usually increase total interest, even if monthly payment is lower.

Can I compare this with other borrowing options?

Yes, use both Loan Calculator and Mortgage Calculator for broader planning.
