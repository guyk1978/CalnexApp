FAQ

Common questions

Are you a bank or a lender?

No. CalnexApp is a publisher of calculators and articles. Nothing here is an offer of credit, and we do not pull your credit or talk to lenders on your behalf.

Will my bank show the same numbers?

Often close on the core payment math—differences usually come from day-count conventions, fees, escrow, insurance, rounding, or promotional rates. Use our output as a baseline, then validate against your Loan Estimate or disclosure.

What do you do with my inputs?

Calculations run in the page unless you explicitly share a link or export. We still recommend treating sensitive numbers like any other personal finance document.
