FAQ

Common questions

Is CalnexApp a lender?

No. We publish calculators and educational content. We do not originate loans or provide individualized financial advice.

How accurate are the estimates?

Outputs depend on the numbers you enter and standard formulas. Lenders may apply fees, insurance, taxes, and underwriting rules not modeled here.

Do I need an account?

You can use the calculators without signing up. If we add saved workspaces in the future, we will state it clearly on those flows.
